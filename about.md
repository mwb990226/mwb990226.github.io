# About EditVerity

EditVerity Sciences, Inc. was founded to address a critical bottleneck  
in genome editing:

**Understanding what genome edits actually produce,  
with sufficient depth, accuracy, and regulatory relevance.**

---

## Our mission

To make genome editing outcomes transparent, interpretable, and safe —  
accelerating the path from discovery to the clinic.

---

## Our background

EditVerity is founded by scientists with deep expertise in:

- Genome editing  
- DNA damage and repair  
- Next-generation sequencing  
- Translational and IND-enabling research  

Our work bridges fundamental science and real-world regulatory needs.
